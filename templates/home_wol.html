AHA: World of Lore

World of Lore is a living text-based world in the ACK! tradition — a new game built on a custom codebase, sub-branded under the ACKmud Historical Archive effort. It carries forward the world, the lore, and the craft of the ACK! lineage into a new era.

Historical Archive

The ACKmud Historical Archive preserves the full ACK! lineage — areas, help libraries, spell references, lore, stories, and the ACK!TNG servers in their final release.
